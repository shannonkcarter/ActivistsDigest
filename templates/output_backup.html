About

This application was created by Shannon Carter during the Insight Data Science fellowship in the fall of 2019. The goal is to provide a means for casual activists to search the entire record of City Council meetings. The app uses word embeddings to allow users to search all public comments from the last 4 years of City Council meetings with a single query
