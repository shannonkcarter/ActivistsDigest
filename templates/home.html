About

This application was created by Shannon Carter during the Insight Data Science fellowship in the fall of 2019. The goal is to provide a means for casual activists to quickly search City Council meeting documentation for a particular topic of interest. The app uses word embeddings to match user input to all public comments made during the last four years of City Council meetings
